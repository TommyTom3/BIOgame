Welcome to BIOgame

BIOgame is currently being created. We aim to provide an interactive study tool for High School Biology students. The "Games" tab will eventually provide a list of topics. Clicking one of these topics will load a game space where you are reading this text. The "Resources" tab will also load in this space. Here you will be able to find links to free online resources. We want to make studying fun, interactive, and collaborative.
